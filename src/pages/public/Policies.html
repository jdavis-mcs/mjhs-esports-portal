Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how the Esports Portal collects, uses, and protects student information. We are committed to protecting student privacy and complying with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA).

1. FERPA Compliance & "School Official" Designation

This service operates under the direct control of the school/district with respect to the use and maintenance of education records. We function as a "School Official" with a legitimate educational interest in the data collected. We do not sell, share, or rent student data to third parties.

2. Information We Collect

In accordance with FERPA, we strictly limit data collection to "Directory Information" required for the operation of tournaments and team management:

  • Student Name (First and Last)
  • School Email Address (for authentication)
  • Grade Level (for bracket sorting)
  • In-Game Usernames (Gamertags)
Note: We do NOT collect home addresses, social security numbers, biometric data, or financial information.

3. No Third-Party Tracking

To ensure student safety, this portal does not utilize third-party advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or marketing scripts. Student browsing behavior is not tracked for commercial purposes.

4. Data Security

All data transmitted to and from this portal is encrypted using Transport Layer Security (HTTPS). Data is stored securely and access is restricted to authorized faculty and student administrators.

Terms of Service

By accessing this Esports Portal, you agree to comply with these Terms and your school's existing Acceptable Use Policy (AUP).

1. Code of Conduct (CIPA Compliance)

To comply with the Children's Internet Protection Act (CIPA), the following behavior is strictly prohibited:

  • Using obscene, profane, or sexually explicit language.
  • Harassment, cyberbullying, or hate speech.
  • Sharing links to harmful or blocked external content.

Violation of these rules will result in immediate account suspension and reporting to school administration.

2. Chat & Moderation

This portal allows for text-based communication for the purpose of team coordination. All communication is logged and subject to review by faculty sponsors to ensure safety and compliance.

3. Eligibility

Access to this portal is a privilege, not a right. It is restricted to currently enrolled students. Accounts may be revoked at any time by school administration.

Questions regarding these policies should be directed to the Faculty Esports Sponsor.
